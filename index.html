    Future tense



    Simple future

    Subject  +   will/shall   +   v

    Use
    • To denote a future action
      • I ,We Shall
      I ,We ,He , She , They Will

      Conditions

      There are conditional Clauses in English. They are probable condition, improved condition and impossible condition.

      Propable condition

      In a probable condition the condition clause in Simple present and the following sentence becomes will + v1(or may+ v1 ,shall + v1 +could + v1)

      Impropable Condition

      In an improbable condition,The conditional Clause is in simple past and the following sentence becomes. would+ v1(might + v1, should + v1 ,could + v1)

      Impossible Condition

      In an impossible condition the conditional Clause becomes past perfect and the following sentence should be would have + v3 (might/could/should have + v3)
