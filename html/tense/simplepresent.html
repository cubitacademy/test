    • Here,we write grammer
  • To denote a Habitual Action
    • I attend Classes thrice a week
  • To denote Universal truth
    • The sun rises in the east
  • To denote general fact
    • Cow eat grass
  • To denote possesion,belongings and ownerships
    • She belongs to me
